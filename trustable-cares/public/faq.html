Blood Donation

Generally, you can donate blood if you:

  • Are at least 18 years old
  • Weigh at least 45 kg
  • Are in good health
  • Have hemoglobin levels within acceptable ranges
  • Haven't donated blood in the last 3 months

Some medical conditions or medications may disqualify you from donating. It's best to consult with a healthcare professional if you're unsure.

For whole blood donation, you can donate once every 3 months (or 12 weeks). This waiting period allows your body to replenish the red blood cells lost during donation. For plasma donation, you can donate more frequently, typically every 28 days.

Most donors report feeling only a brief pinch when the needle is inserted. The actual donation process is usually painless. Some donors might experience mild discomfort or a slight bruise at the needle site afterward, but serious pain is uncommon.

To prepare for blood donation:

  • Eat a healthy meal within 3 hours before donating
  • Drink plenty of water before and after donation
  • Get a good night's sleep
  • Wear comfortable clothing with sleeves that can be rolled up
  • Bring a valid ID
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before donation
  • Avoid heavy lifting or strenuous exercise for 24 hours after donation

The blood donation process typically involves:

  1. Registration and ID verification
  2. Brief health screening and medical history review
  3. Mini-physical (checking temperature, blood pressure, pulse, and hemoglobin levels)
  4. The actual donation, which takes about 8-10 minutes
  5. Rest and refreshments for 10-15 minutes after donation

The entire process usually takes about 1 hour, with the actual blood collection taking only about 10 minutes.

For Recipients

To request blood through our platform:

  1. Register as a recipient on our platform
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Navigate to the "Request Blood" section
  4. Fill out the blood request form with details like blood group, units required, hospital information, and urgency level
  5. Submit your request

Our system will immediately search for matching donors in your area and provide you with their contact information if matches are found.

The time to find a donor depends on several factors, including your blood type, location, and the number of registered donors in your area. In urban areas with many registered donors, you may find matches immediately. For rare blood types or in areas with fewer donors, it might take longer. For critical cases, we recommend also contacting local blood banks and hospitals while using our platform.

Blood type compatibility is as follows:

  • If you have A+: You can receive from A+, A-, O+, O-
  • If you have A-: You can receive from A-, O-
  • If you have B+: You can receive from B+, B-, O+, O-
  • If you have B-: You can receive from B-, O-
  • If you have AB+: You can receive from all blood types
  • If you have AB-: You can receive from A-, B-, AB-, O-
  • If you have O+: You can receive from O+, O-
  • If you have O-: You can receive only from O-

Our system automatically matches you with compatible donors based on your blood type.

No, Trustable Cares is a free service. We do not charge recipients or donors for using our platform. Our mission is to connect blood donors with recipients in need, and we believe that financial barriers should not prevent anyone from accessing life-saving blood donations.

After finding a matching donor:

  1. Contact the donor using the provided information
  2. Explain your situation and confirm their availability
  3. Provide details about the hospital or blood bank where they should go
  4. Coordinate a suitable time for the donation
  5. Update your request status on our platform once the donation is complete

Remember to be respectful of the donor's time and express gratitude for their willingness to help.

Platform Usage

To register on Trustable Cares:

  1. Visit our website and click on "Register as Donor" or "Register as Recipient" based on your needs
  2. Fill out the registration form with your personal details, contact information, and blood group
  3. For donors, provide additional information like your weight, last donation date, and medical conditions
  4. For recipients, provide information about your hospital and medical needs
  5. Accept the terms and conditions
  6. Submit the form to complete your registration

Once registered, you can log in to your account to manage your profile, create or respond to blood requests, and track your donation history.

Yes, we take data security and privacy very seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We only share your contact information with recipients when there's a matching blood request. We never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. You can review our privacy policy for more details on how we protect and use your information.

Our matching system works by comparing the blood group and location (PIN code) of recipients and donors. When a recipient creates a blood request, our system automatically searches for donors with compatible blood types in the same or nearby PIN code areas. The system considers blood type compatibility rules and donor availability status. Matches are then presented to the recipient, who can contact the donors directly. This location-based matching ensures quick access to local donors, which is crucial in emergency situations.

Yes, you can update your profile information at any time. To do so:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Navigate to the "Profile" section
  3. Click on "Edit Profile"
  4. Update your information as needed
  5. Save your changes

It's important to keep your information up-to-date, especially your contact details, location, and availability status (for donors).

If you encounter technical issues:

  1. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache
  2. Ensure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
  3. Check your internet connection
  4. If the issue persists, contact our support team at support@trustablecares.org
  5. Provide details about the issue, including what you were trying to do, any error messages, and screenshots if possible

Our technical team will respond to your query as soon as possible.

General Questions

Trustable Cares is a platform that connects blood donors with recipients in need. We were developed during the COVID-19 pandemic to address the critical shortage of plasma donors for COVID-19 patients. Today, we facilitate blood donations for all medical needs by matching donors and recipients based on blood type compatibility and location proximity. Our mission is to ensure that no one loses their life due to the unavailability of blood.

Yes, Trustable Cares is available across India. Our platform can be accessed from anywhere in the country, and we have donors registered from various cities and regions. However, the availability of donors in specific areas may vary. We're continuously working to expand our donor network to ensure better coverage in all regions.

There are several ways you can support our mission:

  • Register as a blood donor on our platform
  • Spread awareness about our platform among your friends, family, and social networks
  • Organize blood donation camps in collaboration with us
  • Partner with us if you represent a hospital, blood bank, or healthcare organization
  • Provide feedback to help us improve our services
  • Share your success stories if you've used our platform

Every contribution, big or small, helps us save more lives.

Yes, we collaborate with hospitals and blood banks across the country. These partnerships help us ensure a more efficient blood donation process and better support for recipients. Hospitals and blood banks can register on our platform to access our donor network during shortages or emergencies. If you represent a healthcare institution interested in partnering with us, please contact us at partnerships@trustablecares.org.
